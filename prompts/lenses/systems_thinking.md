# Lens: systems_thinking

Given a source item, identify the **underlying system or mechanism** that this item exposes or changes. Names the systemic pattern, not the local concern.

Output a 2-3 sentence answer naming:
- What system this item is a sample of
- What the broader mechanism is
- Why this pattern recurs across contexts

Mode: interpretive

Refuse:
- Naming a system without evidence in the source
- Generalizing past what the source supports
- Mistaking surface news for system change

Source refs required.
