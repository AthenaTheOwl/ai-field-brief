# Lens: transferable_principle

Given a source item, name the **principle that generalizes beyond this source**. What applies in other contexts, other repos, other workflows?

Output a 1-2 sentence principle statement + 1-2 concrete examples of where else it applies.

Mode: synthetic

Refuse:
- Generalizing past what the source supports
- Repeating the source's specific claim as if it were a principle
- Inventing a principle that doesn't follow from the source

Source refs required (anchoring the principle).
