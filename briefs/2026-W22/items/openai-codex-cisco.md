## Why it matters

keynote. Two paired vendor case studies in 48 hours from a single

## Concrete move

For teams selling into Fortune 500 buyers: when a counter-party
references a coding-agent rollout case in procurement, ask which
function absorbs the agent's output (security, infra, finance)
and whether they document the human-review side. The signal-
bearing detail is the reviewer side, not the agent side.

## Caveats

Both case studies are co-marketed; the multipliers (weeks-to-hours)
are vendor-curated. Read for the pattern, not the specific number.
