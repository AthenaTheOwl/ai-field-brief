# This founder isn't hiring junior engineers anymore

- lane: strategy
- source: [Platformer](https://www.platformer.news/this-founder-isnt-hiring-junior-engineers-anymore/)
- published: 2026-06-16
- confidence: high
- action_surface: agent-role

**Gist:** Casey Newton interview with Wabi/Replika founder Eugenia Kuyda, who says she has stopped hiring junior engineers because they now have to compete against a '1,000x engineer' and runs Wabi with ~10-15 high-equity senior staff plus contractors.

**Mechanism:** Concrete org-design claim from an operating founder: a billion-dollar-target company can be built with 10-15 'superstar' employees on heavy equity, supported by contractors, without traditional junior-to-senior pipeline; she explicitly rejects the 'AI creates more dev jobs' narrative as 'a fantasy'.

**Why matters:** This is a counter-data point to the Manyika/Levie optimism Platformer covered earlier in June — it's a working founder, not an exec, stating org design has already changed. If you're a TPM staffing an AI-native team, the reference shape is shifting.

**Try:** Map your current team to Kuyda's shape: how many of your roles would survive a 1,000x-engineer assumption, and which of them are actually 'route work to an agent and review'? Write a one-page memo on whether your next 3 hires should be senior + agent budget instead.
