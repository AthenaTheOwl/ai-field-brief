# ABC-Bench: agentic biosecurity benchmark with wet-lab validated tasks

- lane: frontier-scout
- source: [arXiv (cs.AI)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.11150)
- published: 2026-06-09
- confidence: high
- action_surface: watchlist

**Gist:** Liu et al. released ABC-Bench (ICML 2026), evaluating LLM agents on three biosecurity-relevant capabilities: writing OpenTrons liquid-handler code, designing DNA fragments for in vitro assembly, and evading DNA synthesis screening.

**Mechanism:** All three tasks scored against expert-human medians; one task validated in wet lab (o4-mini-high scripts produced expected DNA when run on an OpenTrons robot).

**Why matters:** First agent benchmark that closes the loop with physical lab execution instead of paper-graded outputs. Sets a higher bar for what 'agent capability eval' means and tightens the watchlist on dual-use risk.
