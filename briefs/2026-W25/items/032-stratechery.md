**Gist:** Weekly recap tying together the Fable export controls and a Michael Morton interview on e-commerce in the AI era, including Shopify durability, the underperformance of ChatGPT checkout, and autonomous last-mile delivery.

**Mechanism:** Reports that OpenAI's ChatGPT checkout experiment is performing poorly while Shopify holds platform position, and frames Anthropic's behavior through the lens of a company that believes only it should build frontier models, justifying export-control-friendly compliance.

**Why matters:** If you were planning to route commerce through an LLM checkout integration, the data point is that the canonical experiment is underwhelming; the lock-in still sits with the merchant platform, not the assistant surface.

**Try:** Before committing engineering to an in-chat checkout flow, run a two-week A/B with one cohort going through ChatGPT/Claude checkout and one through your existing Shopify storefront; track completion rate and average order value, not just click-through.
