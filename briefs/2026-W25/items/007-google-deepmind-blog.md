# UK planning prototype with DeepMind

- lane: primary-source
- source: [Google DeepMind Blog](https://deepmind.google/blog/unlocking-uk-house-building-with-ai-accelerated-planning/)
- published: 2026-06-16
- confidence: high
- action_surface: workflow

**Gist:** DeepMind and the UK government released a planning-officer prototype that pre-processes applications, cites policy, summarizes consultation letters, and drafts assessment reports.

**Mechanism:** Builds on the Extract tool (rolled out to all English councils earlier in June 2026). Drafts include reasoning and proposed conditions; officer keeps full edit + approve/reject authority. Trials in Barnet, Camden, Dorset; national rollout planned 2027. Target: 50% reduction in decision time.

**Why matters:** A live example of agent-assisted decision support with explicit human-in-the-loop scoping. Useful template for any 'agent drafts, human approves' workflow.

**Try:** Sketch the four-step pipeline (consolidate, cite, summarize, draft) for one internal review process you own; identify which step is highest-value to delegate.
