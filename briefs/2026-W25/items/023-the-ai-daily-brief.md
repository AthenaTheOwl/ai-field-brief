# Your Company Doesn't Need An AI Strategy

- lane: fast-signal
- source: [The AI Daily Brief](https://aidailybrief.beehiiv.com/p/your-company-doesn-t-need-an-ai-strategy)
- published: 2026-06-20
- confidence: medium
- action_surface: eval

**Gist:** Argues, drawing on Satya Nadella's framing, that the durable thing for enterprises is private RL environments + custom evals + institutional memory + owning the learning loop.

**Mechanism:** Concrete examples: Microsoft Frontier Tuning (per-org RL on internal workflows), Copilot Cowork as routing execution layer, Harvey redesigning legal workflows as self-improving human-agent systems.

**Why matters:** Reframes the org-design question from 'pick a model' to 'instrument a learning loop'; this is the framing TPMs will hear repeated in 2026 H2 buying conversations.

**Try:** Pick one recurring workflow at your org and instrument three things this week: an eval tied to a business outcome, a log of human corrections, and an owner for the loop. Skip the model choice for now.
